### Northwood Family Notes
- Harry Edward ("Uncle Ed") and his three sisters had cottages on **Lake of Bays, Ontario**
- Uncle Ed's cottage is now the "Coome's place"
- He built a cottage for his sisters, now the "Landis place"
- Dorothy Northwood was the 1st owner of the Landis cottage
- Sidney and Alfred (M.D.) lived in **Wagon Mound, New Mexico** at one time
- This connection led Fred Holmes Thompson to settle in Wagon Mound
- **A.J. Chevalier** built both the Northwood and Thompson cottages at Lake of Bays

## 9. Unresolved Questions

1. **Donald Malcom Montgomery's ancestry** — No information about his parents, birth,
   death, or family origin appears in any of the six photos. This is the most significant
   gap given the project name.

2. **Norine Eva Rowe's connection to Edward Frost Rowe** — Both carry the Rowe surname.
   Is Norine a daughter, niece, or cousin of Edward Frost Rowe? The Smith–Rowe chart
   (Photo 6) only shows one child (Irene) for Edward and Minnie, but may be incomplete.

3. **Isabella Edna Holmes birth year** — 1878 or 1879? The typed chart with day/month
   precision says 3/10/79 (1879); all other sources say 1878.

4. **"Hattie King" identity** — Is this a man named H. King who married Lizzie Holmes,
   or a woman named Hattie King? The handwritten version uses "H. King."

5. **Isabella Smyth's first husband "Kitchen"** — First name unknown. Married ~1863,
   died ~1870. Only documented in the handwritten chart.

6. **Charles F. Thompson (b. 1827) spouse** — "Wm. Bones" as a wife's name is unusual.
   Possible misreading or unconventional notation.

7. **The Irish Rebellion reference** — Which rebellion? Timing conflicts with Queen
   Victoria's reign (1837+) vs. the 1835 marriage date. May refer to Upper Canada
   Rebellion of 1837–38.

8. **Thompson patriarch identity** — First name, wife's name, and exact Scottish origin
   all unknown. The 1827 will of his son Calvin at Sandlake, N.Y. is the earliest
   documented record.

9. **Parent attribution for Della, Arthur, Helen P.** — Are they children of Charles E.
   Thompson or Albert Hoskins Thompson?

10. **Myrtle (1885) and the 1910–1922 children** — The tree structure is ambiguous about
    which parent line Pauline, Josephine, Aubrey, Burton, Melvin, and Walter belong to.

11. **"Fritz" and "Jimbo" real names** — These are almost certainly nicknames for Fred
    Holmes Thompson's sons. Legal names unknown.

12. **Smith family parents** — Photo 6 is cropped at the top; the parents of the Smith
    siblings (Laura Flossie, Minnie May, Elizabeth, Ethel, Freddie) are not visible.

13. **George Nathan Rab[lee?]** — Name partially cut off in Photo 6. Relationship to
    Jones family unclear.

14. **Dorothy Northwood asterisk** — An asterisk appears next to Dorothy Northwood's name
    in Photo 4. Significance unknown.

15. **Millicent Betts Thompson death date** — Not recorded in any source.

16. **Donald Malcom Montgomery birth/death dates** — Not recorded in any source.
