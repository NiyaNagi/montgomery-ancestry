## 7. Key Narrative Findings

### The Rowe Connection — SOLVED
The mystery of how Norine Eva Rowe connects to Edward Frost Rowe is now definitively answered:

**Edward Frost Rowe → George H. Rowe → Norine Eva Rowe → m. James Eugene Montgomery**

George H. Rowe was a **previously unknown son** of Edward Frost Rowe and Minnie May Smith, born 1919 in Detroit. The family photo chart (Photo 6) only showed daughter Irene, omitting George. George married Evaleen Bardwell in 1941 and had four children, including Norine, who married into the Montgomery family.

### The "Rablee" Mystery — SOLVED
"George Nathan Rab[lee?]" at the bottom of Photo 6 is now understood. Irene Rowe (daughter of Edward Frost Rowe) remarried a man named Rablee after Clyde Sylvester Jones died in 1932. The partially cut-off name at the bottom of the chart is connected to this Rablee family.

### Donald Montgomery — Partially Resolved
Donald was born in 1912 in Detroit, died November 19, 1990 in New Haven, Indiana. He was a newspaper publisher who owned papers in Attica and New Haven, Indiana. His parents remain unknown — the most significant remaining gap.
