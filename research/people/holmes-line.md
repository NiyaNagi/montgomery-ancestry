### Other Notable Thomas Holmes — NOT the Same Person

**Dr. Tecumseh Kingsley Holmes (1839–1930)** was a prominent Chatham physician. He was the son of **Abraham Holmes** and **Jane Louisa Gibson** of Harwich Township — a **completely different Holmes family**. Do not confuse:
- Thomas Holmes (1817–189?) — Mayor, grain merchant, son of Samuel Holmes & Mary Ives, from Salford, Lancashire
- Dr. T.K. Holmes (1839–1930) — Physician, son of Abraham Holmes & Jane Louisa Gibson, born in Harwich Township

Source: https://ckphysiciantribute.ca/doctors/tecumseh-holmes/

Interestingly, Dr. T.K. Holmes's sibling list includes a "Thomas Holmes" — this is a different Thomas Holmes entirely (son of Abraham Holmes, not Samuel Holmes).
