### Northwood Family Notes

- **A.J. Chevalier** built both the Northwood and Thompson cottages at Lake of Bays (family notes — not independently verified).
- **The Northwood-Wagon Mound, NM connection** led Fred Holmes Thompson to settle there. This is corroborated by the obituary of James Holmes Thompson (son of Fred Thompson and Eleanor), who grew up on a ranch outside Wagon Mound, NM (per Legacy.com / Albuquerque Journal).
- **John Northwood's flour mills** in Oshawa, Elora (Ontario), and Manitoba, plus his CPR-related land acquisitions, are confirmed by the CK Physician Tribute biography.
- At Alfred's death in 1943, he was "survived by his wife, Monica, his three brothers and four sisters" — meaning all 7 siblings were alive at that time.
- Both Dr. Alfred and Mrs. Monica Northwood are buried at Maple Leaf Cemetery, Chatham.

## 3. KEY FINDING: Norine Eva Rowe's Relationship to Edward Frost Rowe

### Answer: Norine Eva Rowe is the **GRANDDAUGHTER** of Edward Frost Rowe

The relationship chain is:

```
Edward Frost Rowe (1878–1958)  m.  Minnie May Smith (b. 1883)
    │
    ├── Irene Emma Harriet Rowe (1904–1988)
    │       m. 1926 Clyde Sylvester Jones (1891–1932)
    │       └── Clyde Edward Jones (1930–2016)
    │               m. 1952 Margaret Marie Crissey
    │
    └── George H. Rowe (1919–2007)  ← SON of Edward Frost Rowe
            m. 1941 Evaleen Bardwell (1918–2009)
            │
            ├── Dr. Dale E. Rowe (Kalamazoo, MI)
            ├── Georgene M. Rowe (m. Alan Wright; Albuquerque, NM)
            ├── Edward F. Rowe (m. Stephanie; Prairieville, LA)
            └── **Norine E. Rowe** (m. James Montgomery; Portage, MI)
                    │
                    ├── Haley Norine Montgomery
                    └── Jennifer Lea Montgomery
```

### Evidence

1. **George H. Rowe Obituary (2007, Kalamazoo Gazette):**
   - George H. Rowe, born March 16, 1919 in Detroit, MI; died March 6, 2007 in Albuquerque, NM.
   - "On April 14, 1941, he was united in marriage with the former Evaleen Bardwell"
   - **"George H. Rowe was a son of Edward F. Rowe and Minnie (Smith) Rowe"** — this explicitly establishes the parent-child relationship.
   - Survived by children: "Dr. Dale E. & Ellen Rowe of Kalamazoo, MI; Georgene M. & Alan Wright of Albuquerque, NM; Edward F. & Stephanie Rowe of Prairieville, LA; **Norine E. & James Montgomery of Portage, MI**"
   - Source: https://obits.mlive.com/us/obituaries/kalamazoo/name/george-rowe-obituary?id=13574574

2. **Evaleen Bardwell Rowe Obituary (2009, Kalamazoo Gazette):**
   - Evaleen "Eva" Bardwell Rowe; born October 11, 1918 in Flint, MI (twin daughter of Charles and Ruth (Robinette) Bardwell); died August 1, 2009 in Albuquerque, NM.
   - "Wife of the late George H. Rowe, SSgt US Army"
   - Survived by children: Dale, Georgene, Edward, and Norine.
   - Source: https://obits.mlive.com/us/obituaries/kalamazoo/name/evaleen-rowe-obituary?id=12514795

3. **Naming Pattern Confirmation:**
   - George and Evaleen named their son "Edward F. Rowe" — almost certainly after George's father Edward Frost Rowe, reinforcing the generational link.

### Confidence: **HIGH**

The connection is established by two independent obituary sources, both published in the Kalamazoo Gazette and Flint Journal. Norine Eva Rowe is **NOT** a daughter of Edward Frost Rowe; she is his **granddaughter** through his son George H. Rowe.
