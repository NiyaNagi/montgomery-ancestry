# Photo Transcriptions — Montgomery Ancestry Project

> **Analyst notes:** Six photographs were examined, all depicting typed and/or handwritten
> family tree charts. The documents cover three major interconnected family lines: the
> **Thompson paternal line** (Scottish-American origin), the **Thompson maternal line**
> (Smyth/Irish and Gies Hessen/German lines merging with the Holmes family of
> Chatham, Ontario), and a **Smith–Rowe–Jones** line (likely connected through the Rowe
> surname). Together they converge on the marriage of **Millicent Betts Thompson** to
> **Donald Malcom Montgomery**, whose children and grandchildren form the most recent
> generation shown.
